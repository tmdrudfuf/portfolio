What is kimchijjigae?

Kimchijjigae is a popular Korean dish made with kimchi, pork, tofu, onions, and other ingredients. It's often cooked with older, more flavorful kimchi. Jjigae means stew. This food is seasoned with fermented bean paste or red pepper paste. There are many variations, including tuna, Pacific saury, and even vegan versions. It's typically served hot and enjoyed with rice and side dishes.

Preps

  • Slice the onion and chop the green onion
  • Cut the kimchi
  • Cut the pork into bite-sized pieces
  • And then toss the meat with the sauce

Cooking

  1. Stir-fry the marinated pork in a pot until it's half cooked
  2. And add kimchi and stir-fry
  3. Pour broth, and cover the pot and simmer over low heat until it bubbles gently
  4. Finally, add sliced onions and green onions, and bring to a boil
