What is Bibimbap?

Bibimbap is a popular Korean dish made with rice, vegetables, meat, and a spicy sauce called gochujang. The ingredients are mixed together in a bowl and eaten with a spoon. It's often served with a fried egg on top. Bibim means mixing, and bap means cooked rice. Bibimbap has a long history in Korea and is enjoyed for its simplicity, deliciousness, and cultural significance.There are many different kinds of bibimbap, each with its own unique regional ingredients and preparation methods.
source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bibimbap

Ingredient

Main

  • Ground beef
  • Gochujang
  • Radish
  • Zucchini
  • Spinach
  • Bean Sprout
  • Carrot
  • Eggs
  • Sesame seeds

Sauce

  • Minced Garlic
  • Chopped green onion
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Honey
  • Sesame oil
  • Cooking oil
  • Vinegar

Preps

  • Cut the radish, zucchini, and carrot into julienne
  • Cook rice
  • Wash spinach, and bean sprout

Cooking

  1. Fry ground beef in a pan with oil and add green onions when it's half cooked.
  2. Once the green onions have softened, add gochujang, honey, and sesame oil. And stir it
  3. Fry the radish with green onion and oil in a new pan. And then deglaze the pan with half cup of rice water.
  4. Season with minced garlic, salt, sugar and soy sauce
  5. Fry the zucchini with salt, minced garlic, and sesame oil in new pan.
  6. When it is cooked, add sesame seeds
  7. Boil water with 1t salt in a pot and put spinach into the water
  8. cook for 3 seconds on one side, flip, and cood for another 3 seconds
  9. And then wash it with cold water
  10. Squeeze out all the water before seasoning
  11. Season with salt, minced garlic, sesame oil, and sesame seeds
  12. Boil water with 1t salt in a pot and put bean sprout into the water
  13. When it is cooked, wash them with cold water
  14. Drain all the water and season with salt, minced garlic, sesame oil, and sesame seeds
  15. Fry the carrot with oil in a new pan, when they become to light yellow, season with salt
  16. Put some rice in a bowl, then arrange the vegetables on top. Place a fried egg in the middle and finish with a dollop of gochujang
